# Números Complexos

## Retangular

$$
Z = x + jy
$$

## Polar

$$
Z = r\phase{\phi}
$$

## Exponencial

$$
Z = re^{j\phi}
$$

## Conversão

$$
r = \sqrt{x^2 + y^2}, \ \ \ \phi = \tan^{-1} \frac{y}{x}
$$

$$
x = r\cos \phi, \ \ \ \ y= r\sin \phi
$$

$$
\boxed{r\phase{\phi} = r\cos(\omega t + \phi)} \\  phase\ domain \ to \ time \ domain
$$
